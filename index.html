About Us

Think global, act local.

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all. We believe service to mankind is service to God, and every small act of kindness compounds into lasting change.

From distributing free meals to underprivileged families, to running education drives and women's hygiene awareness camps — we're one of the biggest student-led NGOs in India, with chapters in Kanpur, Ghaziabad and growing.

What We Do

Our Programs

Focused initiatives designed around real, everyday needs.

Free Food Drives

Regular meal distribution for homeless and underprivileged families across our city chapters.

Education Support

Free study material, mentorship and tuition support for children who lack access to schooling.

Health & Hygiene

Free sanitary pad distribution and awareness sessions to support women's health and dignity.

Clothing Donations

Seasonal clothing collection and distribution drives for families facing harsh winters.

Volunteer Programs

Internship and volunteering opportunities for students who want to give back to society.

Relief & Crisis Support

Rapid response support during emergencies, disasters and times of community crisis.
